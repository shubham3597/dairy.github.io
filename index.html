Product Information

PureFact FREE MILK

PureFact Lactose Free Milk with less than 0.01% lactose content, this milk has all the goodness of protein, calcium, vitamins and pre-digested Lactose that helps in easy digestion of milk for lactose intolerant people.
PureFact Lactose Free Milk is manufactured in state-of- art manufacturing facility of PureFact Fed Dairy, DELH. Enzyme Lactase is added to ultra heat treated milk at very high precision so that the natural lactose present in milk is broken down in to glucose and galacatose. This milk is packed aseptically to give PureFact Lactose Free Milk. Hence PureFact Lactose Free Milk is the same milk we consume but devoid of lactose. PureFact Lactose Free Milk is first of its kind in India. It is milk without lactose so that a lactose intolerant person can enjoy milk. It has all the goodness of milk like protein, vitamins, calcium etc. A lactose intolerant person who has not been consuming milk and hence has not been getting nutrition from milk may now get nourishment from Milk.
